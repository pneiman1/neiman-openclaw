## Psychology & Pricing Tactics

### Proven Strategies

1. **Charm Pricing**: $47, $97, $147 (odd numbers convert better than $50, $100, $150)
2. **Anchoring**: Show savings "$299 value, yours for $79"
3. **Scarcity**: "Early bird pricing ends soon" or "Limited spots for support tier"
4. **Guarantee**: "Working bot or money back" reduces risk
5. **Bundle Discount**: "Buy now, get future updates free" (value add)

### Common Price Points (Psychological)
- **$47**: Sweet spot for digital products (feels like $40s, not $50s)
- **$79**: Premium but accessible
- **$97**: Just under $100 psychological barrier
- **$147**: Serious buyers only, premium positioning
- **$197**: High-value, needs strong justification
